## How this blog came to be

Glad you're here. I've been wanting a space of my own to track what I learn, write down what I figure out, and have somewhere to come back to when I forget. This blog is that space.

## What you'll find here

I'll mostly write about three things:

1. **Tech notes**
   - Programming language learnings
   - Frameworks and tools — what works, what doesn't
   - Project experience

2. **Solutions**
   - How I think through problems
   - Patterns that hold up
   - Pitfalls and how I got out of them

3. **Personal growth**
   - How I approach learning
   - Career reflections
   - Productivity tools I actually use

## What I'm hoping for

A blog is half the value. The other half comes from the back-and-forth with readers. I'd love to:

- Trade notes with people interested in similar areas
- Share experience and pick up things I would've missed alone
- Hear what you'd push back on

## Following along

A few ways to stay in the loop:

- RSS
- GitHub
- Comments below each post

Thanks for stopping by — looking forward to the road ahead.
